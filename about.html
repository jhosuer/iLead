About Us

Welcome to our Space

Technology has become a vital part of our everday lives. Since the inception of the world wide web in 1991, its become a serious buzz and has defined the globe in mind-blowing ways. iLead was birth because it believes that with technology the world can truly become better. Not just because technology became a feat or because there are now self-driving cars, hover boards or intelligent robots. But because small-scale businesses can do better at their businesses, medium and large scales too. An individual can become an entreprenuer in minutes with the power of technology. Our aim is to contribute to the efficiency and interconnectedness of the world by maximizing the opportunity to serve our customer base effectively.

Nevertheless, we aren't just a technology firm or body. We believe that at every point in time, technology should be made social. Our goal is to ensure that our society is made global by contributing to the Global Citizenship Education of a singular world. By this, we have chosen to contribute from every job given to us by our customer base, to contribute a certain percentage of that job to support the eradication of illiteracy and malnutrition.
